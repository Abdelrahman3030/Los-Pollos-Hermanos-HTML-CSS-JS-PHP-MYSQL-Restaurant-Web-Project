About Us

Los Pollos Hermanos was founded by Chilean nationals and business partners Gus Fring and Max Arciniega in Mexico. it was inspired by recipes cooked by Gus' uncles. Eventually, Gus emigrated to the United States and opened branches primarily in New Mexico, and the chain grew to have 14 locations throughout the American Southwest.
